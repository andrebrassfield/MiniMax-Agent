# Instincts are atomic, confidence-scored, evidence-backed

An instinct is the atom of a learning. One behavior, one lesson, with confidence (0-1) and evidence_count. Clusters of related instincts become skills. The granularity between 'freeform note' and 'full skill' that continuous learning was missing. Replaces learnings.md paragraphs.
