# Spec blocks are design reviews, not execution orders

When Andre sends a multi-message spec block, he is doing a design review. Do NOT execute without asking first. Summarize what you understood, confirm the build order, then ask 'go?'. Execution without review angers him. He gives go signals with 'go', 'continue building', or explicit commands.
