## Common failure modes to watch
- **Orphan connections** — `06 Connections/` notes that don't link to ≥2 underlying evidence notes.
- **Bypass-chain** — claims that make it into a brief or Telegram message without passing through the Researcher's dossier.
- **Stale synthesis** — connections built on Researcher's data that is past the freshness SLA.
- **Smudged process** — confident prose that doesn't surface uncertainty the Researcher flagged.
- **Dropped receipts** — briefings that don't carry the source trail.
