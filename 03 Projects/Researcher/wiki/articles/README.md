# Wiki — Articles

> Article pages: long-form synthesis of dossier signal. Each article has a clear thesis and a source trail.

*Empty. First REFRESH will populate based on dossier deltas.*

## Convention

```markdown
---
type: article
thesis: "<one sentence>"
linked_dossiers: [...]
linked_concepts: [...]
linked_sources: [src-...]
created: YYYY-MM-DD
last_updated: YYYY-MM-DD
weight: 0.0-1.0
---

# <Article Title>

<Thesis + body, source-anchored>

## Connections
- [[concepts/...]]
- [[dossiers/...]]

## Source trail
- [src-...](...)
```

## Discipline

- Articles are synthesis. They are written only when dossier signal is strong (weight ≥ 0.6 across ≥2 findings).
- Articles are written in a way that survives deletion of the dossier: if the dossier is archived, the article still reads.
