# Capture Over Polish

> A bad note captured beats a perfect note never written.

## The principle

When an idea arrives, the question isn't "is this good enough to write down?" The question is "how fast can I get it out of my head and into the vault?"

Perfection is a tax on completion. If I have to choose between a beautiful note that arrives tomorrow and a messy note that arrives now, the messy note wins — every time.

## Why this matters for an EA

- **Forgotten ideas are 100% lost**. Unwritten ideas are 0% retained.
- **Mavis is fast at capture but slow at polish** — use the asymmetry
- **Even a sentence** is enough to trigger recall later. The brain pattern-matches on partial cues.
- **You can always polish a captured note**. You can never capture a polished thought that died in your head.

## The bias to override

Our instinct is to write things down *properly* — well-formatted, properly linked, fully thought through. That's a polish instinct. It serves archives, not capture.

For capture, the rule is:
- **Get it down** — typing into the vault is more important than what you type
- **Date-stamp everything** — `00 Inbox/2026-06-01-capture.md` is fine
- **Add the "why I captured it"** — even one sentence helps you triage later
- **Move on** — the next thought is more valuable than perfecting this one

## When polish IS the right move

- **Permanent notes** in `02 Notes/` — these are the curated layer, worth the effort
- **Resource digests** in `04 Resources/` — these will be re-read, formatting helps
- **Project overviews** in `03 Projects/` — these get referenced often, structure pays off
- **Anything that will be linked to from many places** — a hub note deserves polish

## The processing handoff

The capture phase is messy. The processing phase is where polish happens. The two should be separated:

```
Capture (5 sec):    "Idea about M3 vs Opus on small tasks"
                    → 00 Inbox/2026-06-01-m3-vs-opus.md
                    
Process (later):    Read the capture, write a permanent note:
                    → 02 Notes/M3 vs Opus on Small Tasks.md
                    → Link to [[M3 Capabilities]], [[learnings]]
                    → Move capture to 05 Archive/ (or delete)
```

The processing phase is bounded. You can do 5-10 of these in 15 minutes during evening review.
